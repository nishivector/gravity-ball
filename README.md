# Gravity Ball

A neon arcade puzzle game where you control gravity to guide the ball to the golden goal. Collect cyan orbs, avoid hazards, and master the art of gravity switching across 10 challenging levels!

## How to Play

**Controls:**
- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** — Switch gravity direction
- **R** — Restart level
- **ESC** — Pause game
- **Touch/Swipe** — Swipe in direction you want gravity to pull

**Objective:**
- Reach the golden goal to complete each level
- Collect cyan orbs for bonus points
- Earn 3 stars by completing levels efficiently!

## Features

- 10 progressive levels
- Neon arcade aesthetic with bloom effects
- Particle effects and ball trails
- Original chiptune music and sound effects
- 3-star rating system
- Mobile/touch support
- Level progress saved automatically
